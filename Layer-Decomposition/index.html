Self-supervised Tumor Segmentation throughLayer Decomposition
Xiaoman Zhang
Weidi Xie
Ya Zhang
[Paper]
[GitHub]

Abstract

In this paper, the objective is to design a self-supervised approach that learns effective representation for tumor segmentation. Our key observation is that tumors are generally characterised independently to the surrounding tissues, which allows to treat each input sample as a composition of tumor and normal organ, thus, we propose to train a model that enables to decompose the input sample into tumor, health organ, along with mask for linear blending.
